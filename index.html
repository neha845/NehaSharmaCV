Ms. Neha Sharma

Remote Sensing & GIS Aficionado!

Ms. Neha Sharma is a Geomatician as well as an Architect, presently working as a Geomatics Program Ambassador and Teaching Associate at CEPT University. She is a recent graduate of M.Tech in Geomatics (2018-2020) from CEPT University, Ahmedabad. She has pursued her Bachelors in Architecture (2013-2018) from Maulana Azad National Institute of Technology Bhopal, an Institute of National Importance established by the Government of India. Her master’s thesis topic was ‘Urban Air Quality Analysis for Ahmedabad – A Spatio Temporal Assessment’ and she has done her undergraduate thesis on ‘Naya Raipur Legislative Assembly’. She has also participated as a student volunteer in CEPT in organizing various international and national training, conferences, and symposiums. Her interests are remote sensing, GIS, atmospheric remote sensing, artificial intelligence, machine learning, deep learning, spatial analysis, programming, data analysis, webGIS and 3D modeling.
